ℹ️ How It Works

  1. Rainfall: Rain falls on the rooftop catchment area
  2. Collection: Water flows through gutters to downpipes
  3. Filtration: First flush and filter remove debris and contaminants
  4. Storage: Clean water is stored in the main tank
  5. Distribution: Water is used for household needs or irrigation
  6. Overflow: Excess water is safely diverted when tank is full
